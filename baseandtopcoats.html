Top vs Base Coat

Top Coats Top coat polishes are the last step in any manicure. These polishes are used to protect the other other layers of polishes that have been painted on. They can be found in glossy and matte formulations.
Base Coats Base coat polishes are usually the first layer of polish that is applied when you paint your nails. These polishes are not mandatory to be worn but will aid in how long your manicure lasts and can lessen the damage of staining from dark colored polishes.
